My projects reflect a strong interest in design for manufacturability (DFM), manufacturing systems, and operational robustness. Across UAV platforms, digital manufacturing tools, and computational research, I have focused on engineering solutions that balance performance with cost, scalability, and real-world deployment constraints.

Digital Inventory & Procurement Management System

Designed a digital inventory and procurement management system to replace manual vendor coordination in a small manufacturing environment. I defined the system architecture and procurement decision logic, including inventory tracking, sales logging, demand trend analysis, and reorder triggers. The system emphasized proactive planning, reduced downtime, and data-driven operational decision-making.

Low-Cost Modular UAV Platform

Designed and fabricated a modular UAV platform optimized for low-cost production, rapid assembly, and field repair. Engineering decisions focused on part-count reduction, simplified load paths, vibration mitigation, and compatibility with CNC machining and additive manufacturing. The platform enabled faster iteration cycles while maintaining reliable flight performance.

Disaster-Resistant Micro-Class UAV (AeroThon)

Led the mechanical design of a UAV intended for disaster-response scenarios, including high-wind operation and rapid deployment. The design prioritized modular, injection-moldable arms, crash-resistant structures, and aluminum shock-absorbing landing gear. Manufacturing choices emphasized fast production, ease of repair, and operational robustness under harsh conditions.

Autonomous UAV – RoboNation SUAS

Served as Manufacturing Lead for an international autonomous UAV competition team. Oversaw fabrication planning, material selection, tolerance-aware structural design, and assembly sequencing. Coordinated procurement and manufacturing trade-offs to achieve repeatable builds and reliable system integration under strict competition timelines.

Industrial CFD Consultancy – Centrifugal Blower Optimization

Conducted CFD-based analysis of a centrifugal blower to achieve a specified pressure rise. Studied flow behavior and dimensional parameter sensitivity to guide geometry refinement. The work focused on practical design improvement under industrial performance constraints.

Computational Modeling of Radiofrequency Ablation (OpenFOAM)

Performed numerical modeling of radiofrequency ablation using OpenFOAM to study bioheat transfer and thermal lesion formation. Built simulation workflows for patient-specific parameterization while focusing on minimizing unintended thermal damage, under faculty supervision.

Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINNs) for Thermal Simulation

Extended prior thermal modeling work by integrating Physics-Informed Neural Networks to accelerate computation and enable faster patient-specific predictions. The focus was on reducing solver time while maintaining accuracy and improving usability of simulation workflows.
