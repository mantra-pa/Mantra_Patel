Key Operational Achievement

The UAV was engineered for rapid field deployment, achieving a complete structural assembly time of under 5 minutes. This capability was enabled through a modular carbon-fiber rod architecture, reduced fastener count, and simplified mechanical interfaces.

Manufacturing & Operations Focus

Design decisions were driven by transport constraints, repeated assembly cycles, and short turnaround times between missions. Emphasis was placed on design-for-manufacturing (DFM), design-for-assembly (DFA), tolerance robustness, and minimizing operational failure points.

Modular Structure

Carbon fiber rod architecture enabling sub-5-minute field assembly without specialized tools.

Reduced Joint Count

Minimized fasteners and connection points to reduce vibration, loosening, and assembly complexity.

Repeated Disassembly

Structural layouts optimized for frequent assembly-disassembly cycles without degradation.

Fast Iteration

Manufacturing methods chosen for rapid turnaround enabling quick design improvements.

Field Repairability

Component-level modularity allowing on-site repairs with minimal tools and time.

Transport Optimization

Compact breakdown configuration for efficient transport and storage between test sessions.
