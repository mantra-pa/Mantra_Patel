Competition Overview

RoboNation SUAS is an international UAV competition that evaluates teams on autonomous mission execution, system integration, and reliability across repeated mission cycles under operational constraints.

My Role & Responsibilities

I served as Vice-Captain and Mechanical Lead, with responsibility for manufacturing strategy and structural design decisions. My focus was on ensuring the UAV could be transported, assembled, repaired, and redeployed efficiently during competition operations.

  • Defined manufacturing workflows and build sequencing
  • Led composite fabrication and structural integration
  • Designed modular interfaces for rapid subsystem replacement
  • Planned assembly and logistics for field deployment

Manufacturing & Operations Focus

Design decisions were driven by transport constraints, repeated assembly cycles, and rapid turnaround requirements between missions. Emphasis was placed on manufacturability, tolerance robustness, and minimizing operational failure points.
