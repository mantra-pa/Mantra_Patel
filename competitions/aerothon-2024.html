AeroThon 2024 - SAE India National Competition

Competition Overview

AeroThon is a national-level aerospace design competition organized by SAE India, focused on developing UAV solutions for disaster-struck environments such as floods and cyclones. The competition emphasizes rapid manufacturability, operational robustness, and real-world deployability rather than purely theoretical performance.

Manufacturing & Operational Focus

The UAV was designed for rapid production and field repair, with emphasis on modular components, simplified joints, and tolerance-robust assembly. The architecture supported operation in light rain and high-wind conditions, enabling repeated missions with minimal downtime.

Modular Components

Snap-together assembly allowing rapid field repairs and component replacement without specialized tools.

Simplified Joints

Reduced fastener count and standardized connections to minimize assembly complexity and failure points.

Tolerance-Robust Assembly

Design accommodates manufacturing variations without compromising structural integrity or performance.

Weather Resistance

Sealed electronics and protected structures enable operation in light rain and high-wind conditions.

Rapid Production

Manufacturing process optimized for speed, enabling quick iteration and replacement part production.

Field Repairability

Modular design enables on-site repairs with minimal tools, maximizing operational uptime.
