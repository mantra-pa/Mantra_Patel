My projects reflect a strong interest in design for manufacturability (DFM), manufacturing systems, and operational robustness. Digital Inventory & Procurement Management System

Designed a digital inventory and procurement management system to replace manual vendor coordination in a small manufacturing environment. I defined the system architecture and procurement decision logic, including inventory tracking, sales logging, demand trend analysis, and reorder triggers.

The system emphasized proactive planning, reduced downtime, and data-driven operational decision-making. It included dynamic reorder point calculation, real-time stock visibility dashboards, and vendor lead-time optimization.

Low-Cost Modular UAV Platform

Designed and fabricated a low-cost, reduced-scale UAV to enable early and frequent testing without the cost and risk of full-scale manufacturing. The laser-cut plywood airframe used a modular, jigsaw-style assembly to allow rapid customization, repair, and reconfiguration.

This platform enabled validation of flight behavior, subsystem integration, and assembly logic before transitioning to larger composite UAVs, reducing development risk and iteration time.

Disaster-Resistant Micro-Class UAV (AeroThon)

Led the mechanical design of a micro-class UAV intended for disaster response scenarios, tackling challenges such as high-wind operation, rugged landing, and rapid field deployment.

The design prioritized injection-moldable arms, crash-resistant fuselage structures, and an aluminum shock-absorbing landing gear. Extensive CAD/FEA analysis informed thickness distribution, load paths, and failure modes.

Autonomous UAV – RoboNation SUAS

Served as Manufacturing Lead for an international autonomous UAV competition team. I planned and executed fabrication strategies, selected materials, and developed tolerance-aware structural designs to meet aggressive competition deadlines.

Responsibilities included assembly sequencing, jig design, interference management, and vendor coordination for custom components. Trade-off decisions were documented to balance strength, weight, and manufacturability.

Industrial CFD Consultancy – Centrifugal Blower Optimization

Conducted CFD-based analysis of a centrifugal blower to achieve a specified pressure rise. Studied flow behavior and dimensional parameter sensitivity to guide geometry refinement under industrial performance constraints.

The analysis involved mesh convergence studies, boundary condition sensitivity, and performance maps across operating regimes. Recommendations were delivered with actionable geometry modification suggestions.

Computational Modeling of Radiofrequency Ablation (OpenFOAM + PINNs)

Developed a physics-based thermal model for radiofrequency ablation using OpenFOAM to study bioheat transfer and thermal lesion formation. To address computational bottlenecks, Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINNs) were integrated into the workflow to accelerate thermal predictions while preserving physical consistency.
