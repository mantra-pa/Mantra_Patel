About

I am a mechanical engineering undergraduate with a strong interest in manufacturing-focused design, system integration, and engineering decision-making under real-world constraints.

My experience spans international UAV competitions, industry-oriented engineering projects, and simulation-driven analysis, where design decisions must balance manufacturability, cost, reliability, and deployment timelines.

I am particularly motivated by hands-on environments that emphasize design–build–test iteration and learning through implementation.
