About

I am a mechanical engineering undergraduate with a strong interest in manufacturing-focused design, system integration, and engineering decision-making under real-world constraints.

Through international UAV competitions, industry-oriented projects, and research-driven simulation work, I have gained experience where design choices must balance manufacturability, cost, reliability, and deployment timelines.

I am particularly motivated by hands-on environments that emphasize design–build–test iteration and learning through implementation.

I am particularly interested in how physics-based simulation and data-driven methods can support practical engineering and manufacturing decisions.
